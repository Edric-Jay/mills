# How to play Mills

Nine Men’s Morris for two players (White and Black). Each has **9 pieces**.

## Goal

Win by either:

1. Reducing the opponent to **2 pieces**, or  
2. Leaving the opponent with **no legal moves**

## Phases

### 1. Placing
- Take turns putting one piece on an empty point.
- If you form a **mill** (three of your pieces in a straight line along a board line), remove one opponent piece.
- You normally cannot remove a piece that is in a mill, unless every opponent piece is in a mill.

### 2. Moving
- After both players have placed all 9 pieces, slide a piece to an **adjacent** empty point (along a line).
- Forming a mill still lets you remove an opponent piece.

### 3. Flying
- When you have only **3 pieces** left, your pieces may move to **any** empty point (not only adjacent ones).

## Controls (this app)

- **Green highlights** = legal points for the current action.
- **Place / remove** — tap a highlighted point.
- **Move** — tap your piece, then a highlighted destination.
- Tap the same piece again to deselect.
- **?** in a match opens these instructions.
- When the game ends, a **Victory** / **Defeat** popup appears (or “White/Black wins” in local 2-player). Use **Play again** to restart.
